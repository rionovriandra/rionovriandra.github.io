NHTSA 2021 Traffic Accidents Data Analysis

This project assignment is the group task that I have to complete after finishing three modules in my data analytics course at Refocus Indonesia. In this project, I am working with four other individuals as a team. Our task is to analyze the factors causing traffic accidents in the United States using data from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) in 2021. The objective is to gain insights and provide recommendations that can be implemented in their future systems to reduce traffic accident rates.

For this project, I utilized a combination of PostgreSQL to explore and discover insights from the data, and Google Spreadsheets to create data visualizations.
